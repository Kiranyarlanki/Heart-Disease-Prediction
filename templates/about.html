Our Mission

HeartCare is dedicated to making heart disease risk assessment accessible, accurate, and immediate for everyone. We leverage cutting-edge machine learning technology to provide instant cardiovascular health insights.

How It Works

Our system uses a sophisticated Random Forest machine learning algorithm trained on comprehensive cardiovascular health datasets. The model analyzes 13 key health parameters to assess your risk of heart disease.

Health Parameters We Analyze

  • Age: Your current age in years
  • Sex: Biological sex (male/female)
  • Chest Pain Type: Classification of chest pain symptoms
  • Blood Pressure: Resting systolic blood pressure
  • Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol levels
  • Fasting Blood Sugar: Blood glucose levels when fasting
  • Resting ECG: Electrocardiogram results at rest
  • Max Heart Rate: Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise
  • Exercise Angina: Chest pain induced by physical activity
  • ST Depression: ECG changes during exercise stress test
  • ST Slope: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment
  • Major Vessels: Number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy
  • Thalassemia: Blood disorder affecting hemoglobin production

Important Disclaimer

Medical Advice Notice

This tool is for educational and informational purposes only. The predictions provided by HeartCare should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals regarding any medical concerns or before making any decisions related to your health or treatment. If you have symptoms of heart disease or other medical emergencies, seek immediate medical attention.

When to Seek Medical Help:
  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Swelling in legs or feet
Emergency Situations:
  • Severe chest pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Severe dizziness
  • Suspected heart attack
